KidsZone App Privacy Policy

This privacy policy applies to the use of the KidsZone app available on the Play Store for children under 13.

Information Collection and Use

We do not collect, store, or share any personal information from users, especially from children under the age of 13.

Permissions

The KidsZone app may request certain permissions on the device, but these are solely for the purpose of providing essential functionalities within the app. We do not use these permissions to collect any personal information.

Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA)

We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). KidsZone is specifically designed for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect any personal information from them.
